🌷 Room 3: Tulip Room

1. Every Time I Say Your Name
You bloom in syllables. Soft, sudden, unforgettable — like tulips do in spring.

2. The Color You Are
If red is for love, and yellow for light — then you're the tulip that mixed both and called it us.

3. Bent Toward the Sun
You never chase the spotlight. But somehow, it follows you — like tulips leaning toward what feels right.

4. On Quiet Days
You don’t need to speak to grow. You stand still. And that’s when I notice how deeply you’ve taken root in me.

5. If I Were a Gardener
I’d plant thousands of tulips. But I’d still walk right past them — because no bloom could match what I already have.
